White Place Marghazar Swat

Marghuzar, or Marghazar is a hill station located in the Swat District of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan, and is 13 kilometers away from Saidu sharif.

  • There are 24 active spacious decorated rooms and the Royal Suite (ex-King's bedroom) where Elizabeth II, Queen of the United Kingdom, and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh stayed for three days in 1961.
  • There are two big conference halls where King Abdul Wadood used to hold cabinet meetings.
  • There is the King's lobby which has a beautiful view of the garden lawn.
